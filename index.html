Oğuzhan Fatih Kar

I am a first year Ph.D. student in computer science at École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL). I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in electrical engineering at Middle East Technical University, where I have worked with Figen S. Oktem. My work has focused on novel techniques for computational spectral imaging.

I am interested in computational imaging, deep learning, inverse problems, compressive sensing, and optimization.
