Oğuzhan Fatih Kar

I am a first year Ph.D. student in computer science at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology (EPFL), where I am advised by Amir Zamir. I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in electrical engineering at METU, where I have worked with Figen S. Oktem. My M.S. work had focused on developing novel reconstruction techniques for computational imaging.

My current research interests are in building robust and general visual perception systems that can operate in the real world.
